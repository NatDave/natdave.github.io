Tutored practical applications of AutoCAD Civil 3D for highway design projects, including horizontal and vertical alignment creation and editing, corridor modeling and assembly design, earthwork calculations and grading optimization, profile and cross-section generation, superelevation analysis and application, and quantity takeoff reports for construction documentation.
