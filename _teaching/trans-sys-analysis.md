Graded assignments covering urban transportation modeling and policy analysis, including travel demand forecasting and four-step modeling processes, traffic flow theory and capacity analysis, mode choice analysis and transit planning strategies, land use and transportation integration, network optimization and route assignment methods, and evaluation of transportation projects through economic and environmental impact assessments.
